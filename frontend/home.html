Awareness on Document Originality

In today's digital age, document forgery has become a serious issue. It is crucial to ensure the authenticity of any document before relying on it for legal, financial, or academic purposes. Fake documents can lead to fraud, identity theft, and severe legal consequences. Our document verification system is designed to help individuals and organizations validate documents efficiently and securely.

How to Verify Your Document

  1. Go to the Verify page.
  2. Upload your document in the specified format (PDF, JPEG, PNG).
  3. Click the "Verify" button to initiate the validation process.
  4. Wait for the system to analyze and cross-check the document.
  5. Receive verification results indicating whether your document is authentic or requires further review.
